How is your Noggin

How well do you know today’s NFL stars? Over the years, the game has changed, shifting from a hard-hitting gladiator sport to a safer, more mindful version.
This is due to the growing awareness of concussions and their long-term effects on players, impacting their lives even after football.
This memory game is a tribute to those affected by CTE and other concussion-related challenges.
Let’s honor the legends and Hall of Famers by testing your football knowledge.
So, how sharp is your memory? Ready to challenge your noggin?

Instructions:

This NFL-themed memory game challenges players to match current NFL players hidden behind a grid of squares.
Players will select squares, trying to find matching pairs within 8 attempts. Each round consists of 12 squares, and you'll have 16 seconds to make your selections.
With each new round, the time to complete your matches decreases, raising the stakes.To win, you must successfully match three pairs in a row.
Each NFL player will have a voice over if the matches are correct!

Good Luck!
